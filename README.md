# 🧠 RankSense AI — AEO Diagnostic Engine (Gemini Edition)

> If your brand isn’t recommended by AI, it doesn’t exist.

RankSense AI helps you understand how AI assistants recommend products—and whether your brand shows up at all.

---

## 🚀 Problem

AI tools like ChatGPT and Gemini are replacing traditional search.

When users ask:
> "best magnesium supplement for seniors"

They get **AI-generated recommendations**, not search results.

👉 Brands have zero visibility into:
- Whether they are recommended
- Which competitors dominate
- Why they are missing

---

## 💡 Solution

RankSense AI simulates real AI buying behavior using **Google Gemini**.

It runs the same query across **3 buyer personas**:
- 💰 Budget-conscious shopper  
- 💎 Premium buyer  
- 🧠 Domain expert  

Then analyzes how brands perform across these perspectives.

---

## ⚙️ How It Works

1. User enters a product query  
2. Gemini runs 3 times with different personas  
3. Each persona returns top 5 ranked brands  
4. System extracts and analyzes:
   - Rankings
   - Brand frequency
   - Visibility score  
5. Outputs insights + suggestions  

---

## 📊 Features

### 🏆 What AI Actually Recommends
- Leaderboard of top brands
- Based on ranking position + frequency
- Visibility Score (0–100)

### 👥 Persona-Based Rankings
- Compare how different buyers evaluate products

### 🎯 Brand Tracking
- Enter your own brand
- Check if it appears in AI recommendations
- Detect **zero visibility risk**

### 📈 Insights Engine
- Identifies dominant brands
- Detects market gaps
- Shows niche vs universal appeal

### 🧠 Why You're Not Ranking
- AI-generated explanation
- Actionable improvement suggestions
